These briefs provide the factual foundation for:

## Standards

- Every factual claim has an inline citation
- [NEEDS VERIFICATION] marks anything uncertain
- No editorial spin — present facts and let readers draw conclusions
- Sources include federal statutes, CRS reports, GAO findings, OGE documents, and published reporting from ProPublica, NOTUS, CNBC, Bloomberg, Campaign Legal Center, CREW, and others
