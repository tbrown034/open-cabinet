## Core Differentiator

ProPublica hosts raw PDF documents on DocumentCloud. Users must read the PDFs themselves to find individual transactions.

Open Cabinet extracts every transaction into structured JSON: description, ticker, type, date, amount range, late filing flag. Searchable, sortable, visualizable.
